Automations

Brand-level controls, franchise enforcement

New Brand-level Automation Controls
Portal admins with franchise permissions can now manage automations and templates at the brand level, ensuring every location follows the same playbook. When enabled, automation settings are enforced and applied consistently across all studios.

ClubReady Integration

Opt-in/out, sync, form notes

Improved SMS Opt-In & Opt-Out Handling
Overhauled how Loyalsnap handles ClubReady opt-in/opt-out data, addressing a major source of support tickets.
  • Portal users can now re-opt-in clients marked as "hard opted out" in ClubReady by confirming consent
  • Transactional messages (appointment confirmations, reminders) are no longer blocked by inaccurate ClubReady opt-out data
  • Marketing messages continue to respect opt-out preferences
New Communication Preferences Sync
Loyalsnap now processes ClubReady's communication preferences webhooks, so opt-in and opt-out changes sync faster between systems.
New Form Submission Notes
Form responses are now automatically posted as a note in ClubReady with a link to the form — giving staff immediate visibility without needing Loyalsnap access.
